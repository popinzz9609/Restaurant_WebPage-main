About Us

Based in Coimbatore, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family owned restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Little Lemon is owned by two brothers, Prashanth and Naveen, who moved to the Tamil Nadu to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Prashanth relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Tamil Nadu.

Naveen does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional chinese from the mediterranean region.
